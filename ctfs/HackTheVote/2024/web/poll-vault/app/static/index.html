Case Studies

Our Election Forecast platform has successfully predicted key election outcomes in previous cycles, both in Georgia and across the nation. Learn how our data-driven insights influenced campaign strategies, media coverage, and public perception.

Case Study 1: The 1948 Three Governors Controversy

Georgia unexpectedly found itself with three simultaneous claimants to the governor's office in 1948. Our platform's predictive insights were invaluable for citizens trying to understand who was really in charge, helping to demystify one of Georgia's most bizarre political sagas.

Case Study 2: The 1985 Wild Turkey Election

Our Election Forecast platform provided critical insights during Georgia's "turkey tally," allowing voters to track the hunting-based "results" in real time. Our unique data visualizations even predicted which side would bag the most turkeys, ensuring accurate results in one of Georgia's wildest elections.

Case Study 3: The 1922 Goat Vote

In an unexpected twist, our platform followed the legendary Georgia "Goat Vote" in 1922, where a candidate of the four-legged variety gave traditional politicians a run for their money. Our data tracked Billy's grassroots surge, showing that a little humor—and a goat—could make a big impact in local politics.
